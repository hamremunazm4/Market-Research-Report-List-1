<p><strong>The Acrylic Panel Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Extruded Acrylic Panel</li><li>Cast Acrylic Panel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The acrylic panel market features two main types: extruded acrylic panels and cast acrylic panels. Extruded acrylic panels are produced through a manufacturing process that shapes heated acrylic resin into a continuous sheet, offering consistent thickness and lower cost, making them suitable for various applications. In contrast, cast acrylic panels are made by pouring liquid acrylic into molds, resulting in superior optical clarity, strength, and a wider range of thicknesses, making them ideal for high-end applications where quality is paramount.</p></p>
