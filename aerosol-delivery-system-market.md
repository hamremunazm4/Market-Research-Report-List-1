<p><strong>The Aerosol Delivery System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Small-volume Nebulizer (SVN)</li><li>Pressurized Metered-dose Inhaler (pMDI)</li><li>Dry Powder Inhaler (DPI)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aerosol delivery system market is primarily categorized into three types: Small-volume nebulizers (SVNs), which transform liquid medication into aerosol for inhalation; Pressurized metered-dose inhalers (pMDIs), which deliver specific doses of medication in aerosol form with a propellant; and Dry powder inhalers (DPIs), which contain medication in powder form and rely on patient inhalation for drug delivery. Each device type caters to different patient needs, preferences, and therapeutic requirements in respiratory care.</p></p>
