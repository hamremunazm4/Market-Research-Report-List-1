<p><p>An Aircraft Health Monitoring System (AHMS) enhances aircraft safety and efficiency by continuously assessing the condition of various systems. The market is divided into several types: </p><p>1. **Diagnostics** involves identifying current faults within systems.</p><p>2. **Prognostics** predicts potential failures, enabling proactive maintenance.</p><p>3. **Condition-Based Maintenance (CBM)** emphasizes maintenance based on actual system condition rather than on a fixed schedule.</p><p>4. **Adaptive Control** utilizes real-time data to adjust operating parameters for optimal performance and safety.</p></p>
