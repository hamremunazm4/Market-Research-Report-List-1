<p><p>The airplane boarding stairs market consists of two main types: towable passenger stairways and chassis-mounted passenger stairways. Towable passenger stairways are portable and can be easily connected to a vehicle for transport, offering flexibility and convenience at various airports. In contrast, chassis-mounted passenger stairways are built directly onto a vehicle chassis, providing a stable and integrated solution for boarding and deplaning passengers. Both types enhance passenger accessibility to aircraft, catering to different operational needs and airport configurations.</p></p>
