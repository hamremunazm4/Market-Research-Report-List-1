<p><p>Automated Optical Inspection (AOI) for flexible OLED displays encompasses two primary market types: inline and offline AOI equipment. Inline AOI equipment is integrated into the production line, allowing real-time inspection during the manufacturing process to ensure quality, reduce defects, and enhance efficiency. In contrast, offline AOI equipment operates independently of the production line, enabling thorough inspections of completed products. Both types aim to maintain high standards in display quality while minimizing production disruptions and enhancing overall yield.</p></p>
