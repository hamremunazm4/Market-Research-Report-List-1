<p><p>Axial flow impeller pumps are categorized into horizontal and vertical types based on their orientation and application. Horizontal pumps are designed to operate in a horizontal position, offering ease of maintenance and installation, making them suitable for a wide range of applications like irrigation and industrial processes. Vertical pumps, on the other hand, are utilized where space is limited and can handle higher capacities, often used in deep well pumping. Both types efficiently move fluids in various industries, including water treatment and chemical processing.</p></p>
