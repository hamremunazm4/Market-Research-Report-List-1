<p><strong>The Bicycle Motors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hub Motor</li><li>Crank Motor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bicycle motors market comprises primarily two types: hub motors and crank motors. Hub motors are integrated into the wheel hubs, providing direct propulsion and offering a simple installation with low maintenance. They are popular for their ease of use and are commonly found in urban commuting e-bikes. Crank motors, or mid-drive motors, are mounted near the bike's crankshaft, delivering power through the bike's gears for improved hill climbing and efficiency. This type is favored by performance-oriented cyclists for its balance and handling.</p></p>
