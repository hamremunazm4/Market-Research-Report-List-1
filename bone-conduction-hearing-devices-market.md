<p><strong>The Bone Conduction Hearing Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Traditional Bone Conduction Hearing Aids</li><li>Bone Anchored Hearing Aids</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bone conduction hearing devices market encompasses two primary types: Traditional Bone Conduction Hearing Aids and Bone Anchored Hearing Aids (BAHA). Traditional Bone Conduction Hearing Aids transmit sound vibrations directly through the skull, suitable for those with mild to moderate hearing loss. In contrast, Bone Anchored Hearing Aids utilize a surgically implanted titanium fixture to transmit sound through the bone, addressing conductive hearing loss or single-sided deafness, offering a more permanent solution with improved sound quality and comfort.</p></p>
