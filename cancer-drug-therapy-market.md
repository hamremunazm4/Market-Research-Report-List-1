<p><p>The cancer drug therapy market encompasses several key types: targeted therapy, chemotherapy, and immunotherapy. Targeted therapy focuses on specific molecular targets associated with cancer, aiming to disrupt tumor growth with minimal damage to healthy cells. Chemotherapy employs cytotoxic drugs to kill rapidly dividing cells, though it can affect healthy tissues as well. Immunotherapy harnesses the body’s immune system to recognize and combat cancer cells, enhancing the body’s natural defenses. Each approach offers unique benefits and is often used in combination to improve treatment outcomes.</p></p>
