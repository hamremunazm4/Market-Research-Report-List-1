<p><ul><li>Autologous Chondrocyte Implantation</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cartilage regeneration technology market is primarily divided into two types: Autologous Chondrocyte Implantation (ACI) and other methods. ACI involves harvesting a patient's own cartilage cells, culturing them, and then implanting them back into the damaged area, promoting natural cartilage growth. Other techniques encompass alternatives like stem cell therapy, tissue engineering, and biomaterial scaffolds that support cartilage repair. These approaches aim to restore joint function and alleviate pain, catering to diverse patient needs and types of cartilage injuries.</p></p>
