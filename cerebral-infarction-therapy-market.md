<p><strong>The Cerebral Infarction Therapy Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Partial Anterior Circulation Infarct (PACI)</li><li>Total Anterior Circulation Infarct (TACI)</li><li>Posterior Circulation Infarct (POCI)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cerebral infarction therapy market is segmented into three main types based on the area of the brain affected: Partial Anterior Circulation Infarct (PACI), Total Anterior Circulation Infarct (TACI), and Posterior Circulation Infarct (POCI). PACI involves limited areas of the anterior circulation, typically resulting in mild deficits. TACI affects the entire anterior circulation, leading to significant neurological impairment. POCI impacts the posterior circulation, affecting brainstem and cerebellar functions, and requires targeted therapies for recovery and rehabilitation.</p></p>
