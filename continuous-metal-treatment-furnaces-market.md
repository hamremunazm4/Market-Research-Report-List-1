<p><p>Continuous metal treatment furnaces are essential for various metallurgical processes, including heat treatment and annealing. Belt furnaces use a moving conveyor system to process materials uniformly. Rotating hearth furnaces feature a circular platform that rotates, ensuring even heat distribution for parts with complex shapes. Pusher furnaces utilize a series of pusher mechanisms to move metal through the heating chamber, ideal for high-volume production. Other types include chamber and induction furnaces, each tailored for specific applications in metal processing.</p></p>
