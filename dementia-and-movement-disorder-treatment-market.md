<p><p>The dementia and movement disorder treatment market consists of various pharmacological approaches. MAO inhibitors, which block the activity of monoamine oxidase, aim to increase neurotransmitter levels, improving mood and cognitive function. AChE inhibitors enhance acetylcholine availability, supporting memory and learning in dementia patients. Glutamate inhibitors target the glutamate neurotransmitter system, reducing excitotoxicity and offering neuroprotective effects. Together, these treatments address cognitive decline and motor symptoms associated with disorders like Alzheimer's and Parkinson's disease, enhancing patient quality of life.</p></p>
