<p><p>The dispensary POS software market is primarily divided into two types: cloud-based and on-premise solutions. Cloud-based POS systems operate on remote servers, offering flexibility, real-time updates, and accessibility from any internet-connected device. This option often reduces maintenance costs and simplifies integration. Conversely, on-premise systems are installed locally on the dispensary's hardware, providing greater control over data and security but requiring more upfront investment and ongoing maintenance. Each type has its advantages, catering to different business needs and operational preferences.</p></p>
