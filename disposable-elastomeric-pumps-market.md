<p><p>The disposable elastomeric pumps market is segmented into two main types: continuous rate elastomeric pumps and variable rate elastomeric pumps. Continuous rate elastomeric pumps deliver a steady flow of medication at a preset rate, ideal for consistent therapies such as post-operative pain management. In contrast, variable rate elastomeric pumps allow for adjustable flow rates, accommodating varying patient needs and responding to different treatment protocols. Both types provide convenience and reduce the risk of infection by eliminating the need for complex tubing and infusion devices.</p></p>
