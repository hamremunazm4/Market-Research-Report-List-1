<p><p>Distributed Antenna Systems (DAS) enhance cellular coverage by utilizing multiple antennas connected to a central source. There are three primary market types: Active DAS, which employs powered antennas for signal amplification and distribution; Passive DAS, which uses coaxial cables to distribute signals without amplification; and Hybrid DAS, combining both active and passive components for optimal performance. Each type caters to different coverage needs and environments, ensuring reliable connectivity in challenging spaces such as large buildings or urban areas.</p></p>
