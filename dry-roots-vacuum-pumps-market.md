<p><ul><li>Single Stage</li><li>Multi Stage</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dry roots vacuum pumps are categorized into two main types: single-stage and multi-stage. Single-stage pumps use a single rotor chamber to create vacuum pressure, making them suitable for applications with moderate vacuum requirements. Multi-stage pumps, on the other hand, consist of multiple rotor chambers arranged in series, allowing them to achieve higher vacuum levels and greater efficiency. The choice between these types typically depends on the specific application demands, with multi-stage pumps favored for more rigorous operational environments.</p></p>
