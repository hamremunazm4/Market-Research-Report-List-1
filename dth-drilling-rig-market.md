<p><p>The DTH drilling rig market can be categorized into three main types: crawler, portable, and tripod-style rigs. Crawler rigs are characterized by their tracked systems, providing stability and mobility on rugged terrains, making them suitable for large-scale projects. Portable rigs are lightweight and easily transportable, ideal for smaller sites or temporary operations. Tripod-style rigs utilize a three-legged support structure, allowing for quick setup and versatility in confined spaces, making them suitable for varied drilling applications.</p></p>
