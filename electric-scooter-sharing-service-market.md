<p><p>Electric scooter sharing services can be categorized into two main types: dockless and station-based. Dockless systems allow users to pick up and drop off scooters anywhere within a designated area, offering greater flexibility and convenience. In contrast, station-based systems require users to locate and return scooters at specific docking stations, ensuring better organization and management of vehicles. Each model has its benefits, with dockless systems promoting spontaneous use and station-based systems enhancing accountability and maintenance.</p></p>
