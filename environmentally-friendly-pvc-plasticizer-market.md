<p><p>The environmentally-friendly PVC plasticizer market includes several types: Adipates esters, which enhance flexibility and thermal stability; Trimellitates esters, known for their low volatility and high-temperature performance; Epoxies esters, offering improved chemical resistance and compatibility; and Benzoates, which provide low temperatures and light stability. These plasticizers are formulated to reduce environmental impact while maintaining the desired properties of PVC products, making them suitable for applications in various industries, including automotive, construction, and consumer goods.</p></p>
