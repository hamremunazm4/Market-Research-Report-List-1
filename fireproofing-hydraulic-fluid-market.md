<p><strong>The Fireproofing Hydraulic Fluid Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>HFA</li><li>HFB</li><li>HFC</li><li>HFD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fireproofing hydraulic fluid market is categorized into four main types: HFA, HFB, HFC, and HFD. HFA fluids are water-containing emulsions, offering good fire resistance. HFB fluids are also emulsions but with higher anti-corrosion properties. HFC fluids are water-containing and have a lower flash point, providing excellent fire protection for high-temperature applications. HFD fluids are non-water-containing synthetic oils designed for high performance in extreme conditions, ensuring superior fire resistance and lubrication in hydraulic systems.</p></p>
