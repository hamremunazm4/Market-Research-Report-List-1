<p><ul><li>Active Claims</li><li>Passive Claims</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The flight delay insurance market can be segmented into active and passive claims. Active claims refer to situations where policyholders experience immediate flight delays and file claims for compensation due to unexpected disruptions, such as weather or technical issues. In contrast, passive claims involve scenarios where customers may not actively pursue compensation but are eligible for it based on policy stipulations, often triggered by systemic issues or travel disruptions impacting multiple flights. Both types cater to varying traveler needs.</p></p>
