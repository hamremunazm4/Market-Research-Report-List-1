<p><p>The Glass CNC Machining Center market is divided into vertical and horizontal types based on the orientation of the machine. Vertical CNC machining centers feature a vertical spindle, allowing for efficient processing of glass sheets in a compact space, ideal for intricate designs and vertical cutting applications. In contrast, horizontal CNC machining centers utilize a horizontal spindle, providing greater stability for larger glass sheets and facilitating tasks like drilling and milling. Both types serve diverse needs in the glass processing industry.</p></p>
