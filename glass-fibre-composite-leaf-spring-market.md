<p><p>The Glass Fibre Composite Leaf Spring market is divided into two primary types: transversal and longitudinal. Transversal leaf springs are designed to support loads across their width, providing stability and flexibility, making them suitable for specific vehicle applications. In contrast, longitudinal leaf springs run parallel to the vehicle's length, delivering enhanced strength and load-bearing capacity, ideal for heavy-duty applications. Both designs leverage the lightweight and durable properties of glass fibre composites, improving vehicle performance and efficiency.</p></p>
