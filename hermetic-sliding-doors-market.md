<p><p>Hermetic sliding doors are specialized entry systems designed to create airtight environments, primarily used in cleanrooms, laboratories, and medical facilities. The market comprises two main types: single leaf and double leaf. Single leaf doors feature a single panel that slides open or closed, ideal for limited space and moderate traffic. In contrast, double leaf doors consist of two panels, allowing for wider access and accommodating higher traffic volumes, making them suitable for larger installations where efficiency and hygiene are critical.</p></p>
