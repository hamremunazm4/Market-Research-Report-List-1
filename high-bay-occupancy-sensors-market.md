<p><p>The high bay occupancy sensors market includes three primary types: PIR (Passive Infrared) sensors, Ultrasonic sensors, and Dual-technology occupancy sensors. PIR sensors detect motion through changes in infrared radiation, ideal for detecting heat from people. Ultrasonic sensors use sound waves to sense movement, providing wider coverage in complex layouts. Dual-technology sensors combine PIR and ultrasonic technologies, enhancing accuracy and reducing false triggers. These options cater to various high bay lighting applications, improving energy efficiency and automation in commercial environments.</p></p>
