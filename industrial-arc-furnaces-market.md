<p><strong>The Industrial Arc Furnaces Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>AC-EAF</li><li>DC-EAF</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Industrial arc furnaces are predominantly classified into two types: AC-EAF (Alternating Current Electric Arc Furnace) and DC-EAF (Direct Current Electric Arc Furnace). AC-EAF utilizes alternating current to produce heat through an electric arc, making it suitable for recycling scrap metals and producing steel. In contrast, DC-EAF employs direct current for more efficient energy use and superior control over melting processes, resulting in higher quality steel production. Both types serve critical roles in the steelmaking industry, with varying applications and advantages.</p></p>
