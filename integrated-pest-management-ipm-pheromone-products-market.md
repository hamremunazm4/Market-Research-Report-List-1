<p><p>Integrated Pest Management (IPM) pheromone products are categorized into four main types: </p><p>1. **Sex Pheromones** attract mating partners to disrupt reproduction.</p><p>2. **Aggregation Pheromones** lure insects together, facilitating trapping and monitoring.</p><p>3. **Oviposition Deterring Pheromones** inhibit egg-laying, reducing pest populations.</p><p>4. **Alarm Pheromones** alert insects to danger, influencing behavior to minimize crop damage. </p><p>These products are essential in creating sustainable pest control strategies while minimizing chemical pesticide use.</p></p>
