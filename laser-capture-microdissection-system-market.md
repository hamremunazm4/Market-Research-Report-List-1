<p><p>The Laser Capture Microdissection (LCM) market includes several types based on the light sources used for tissue extraction. Ultraviolet LCM employs UV lasers for precise targeting of cells, while Infrared LCM uses IR lasers for capturing tissue without damaging surrounding areas. Ultraviolet and Infrared LCM combines both techniques for enhanced flexibility. Immunofluorescence LCM, on the other hand, integrates fluorescence detection to visualize specific proteins before microdissection, facilitating targeted analysis of particular cell types or molecular markers.</p></p>
