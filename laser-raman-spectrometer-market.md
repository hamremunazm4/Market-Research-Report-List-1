<p><p>A Laser Raman Spectrometer is an analytical instrument that utilizes the Raman scattering phenomenon to provide detailed information about molecular composition and structure. By focusing a laser beam onto a sample, it measures the scattered light, revealing molecular vibrations that can be correlated with specific chemical bonds.
