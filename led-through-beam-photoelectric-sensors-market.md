<p><p>LED through beam photoelectric sensors are designed to detect objects by emitting a beam of light that is interrupted when an object passes through. The market for these sensors is divided into two main types based on signal output: digital signal and analog signal. Digital signal sensors provide a simple on/off output when the beam is interrupted, ideal for basic applications. In contrast, analog signal sensors offer varying levels of output depending on the intensity of the received light, enabling more nuanced detection and measurement.</p></p>
