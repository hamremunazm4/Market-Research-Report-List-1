<p><p>Mobile shredding trucks are categorized primarily into wheel-type and crawler-type vehicles. Wheel-type trucks are designed for road transportation, offering superior maneuverability and speed, making them ideal for urban shredding services and businesses with limited space. Crawler-type trucks, on the other hand, utilize tracks instead of wheels, providing enhanced stability and traction on rough terrains, which is beneficial for remote locations and larger shredding jobs. Both types address the diverse needs of secure document destruction in different environments.</p></p>
