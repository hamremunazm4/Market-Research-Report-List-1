<p><p>Motorized control valves are essential components in fluid control systems, featuring several types differentiated by design and function. Butterfly valves are known for their disc-shaped body, allowing for quick operation and minimal pressure drop. Ball valves utilize a spherical body for durable, tight sealing and quick shut-off. Globe valves feature a globe-shaped body for precise flow regulation but may have higher pressure drops. Other types include gate and check valves, each serving specific applications in industries such as water management, HVAC, and manufacturing.</p></p>
