<p><ul><li>Gas Proportional Detectors</li><li>Scintillation Detectors</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The neutron detectors market is primarily categorized into three types: gas proportional detectors, scintillation detectors, and other market variants. Gas proportional detectors utilize a gas-filled chamber to measure ionization events caused by neutrons, providing high sensitivity and energy resolution. Scintillation detectors employ materials that emit light when interacting with neutrons, offering fast response times and the capability for spectroscopic measurements. The "other" category encompasses innovative or less common technologies, such as semiconductor detectors and track-etch detectors, broadening the market further.</p></p>
