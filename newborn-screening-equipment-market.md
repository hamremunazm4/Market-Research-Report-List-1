<p><p>The newborn screening equipment market encompasses various types of tests to identify metabolic and genetic disorders in infants. Medium-Chain Acyl-CoA Dehydrogenase (MCAD) deficiency screens for fatty acid oxidation disorders. Tyrosinemia tests for an inability to metabolize certain amino acids.
