<p><ul><li>Electrostatic Filtration</li><li>Frame Filter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Oil Mist Eliminators market comprises primarily two types: Electrostatic Filtration and Frame Filters. Electrostatic Filtration employs charged plates to attract and capture oil mist particles, offering high efficiency and minimal pressure drop. This method is suitable for applications requiring stringent air quality standards. Frame Filters, on the other hand, utilize a mechanical barrier to trap oil mist particles, making them cost-effective and easier to maintain. Both types are essential for ensuring clean air in industrial environments.</p></p>
