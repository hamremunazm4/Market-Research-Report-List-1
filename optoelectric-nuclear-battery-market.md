<p><strong>The Optoelectric Nuclear Battery Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermal Conversion Type</li><li>No-Thermal Conversion Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The optoelectric nuclear battery market can be divided into two main types: thermal conversion and no-thermal conversion. Thermal conversion types use heat generated from radioactive decay to produce electricity, converting thermal energy into electrical energy through various mechanisms, often involving thermoelectric materials. In contrast, no-thermal conversion types directly convert ionizing radiation from radioactive materials into electric power without significant heat generation, typically utilizing photoelectric or similar principles, offering more efficient energy conversion without relying on thermal processes.</p></p>
