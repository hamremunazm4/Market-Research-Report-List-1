<p><p>The osteoarthritis pain solution market can be categorized into three main types: oral, injection, and external treatments. Injection treatments, including corticosteroids and hyaluronic acid, target localized pain directly within the joint, providing faster relief. External solutions involve topical applications like creams and patches, delivering pain relief directly at the site of discomfort. Each type offers unique advantages based on patient needs and severity of symptoms.</p></p>
