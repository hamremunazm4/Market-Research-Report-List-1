<p><p>The paint adhesion promoters market is divided into two main types: waterborne and oil-based paint adhesion promoters. Waterborne paint adhesion promoters are solvent-free, environmentally friendly, and offer excellent adhesion properties for various substrates, making them suitable for indoor applications. In contrast, oil-based paint adhesion promoters provide enhanced performance on challenging surfaces and are ideal for outdoor and industrial applications, delivering superior durability and resistance to weathering. Both types enhance paint adhesion, ensuring long-lasting finishes.</p></p>
