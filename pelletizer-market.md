<p><strong>The Pelletizer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Strand Palletizing System</li><li>Underwater Palletizing System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pelletizer market encompasses various systems designed for transforming materials into pellets, primarily for processing plastics. The strand pelletizing system involves extruding molten material into continuous strands, which are then cooled and cut into pellets. In contrast, the underwater pelletizing system submerges the extruded material in water, enabling effective cooling and shaping before cutting. Both systems cater to different production needs, with strand systems often used for thermoplastics and underwater systems favored for high-viscosity materials, enhancing efficiency and product consistency.</p></p>
