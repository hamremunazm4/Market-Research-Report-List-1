<p><ul><li>Power Management IC (PMIC)</li><li>Driver IC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Power ICs market is primarily categorized into Power Management Integrated Circuits (PMICs) and Driver ICs. PMICs regulate voltage and current, manage battery charging, and optimize power consumption in devices, making them essential in consumer electronics, automotive, and industrial applications. Driver ICs, on the other hand, control the performance of specific components like LEDs, motors, or displays, facilitating efficient operation in devices such as smartphones and appliances. Together, they enhance overall energy efficiency and functionality in modern technology.</p></p>
