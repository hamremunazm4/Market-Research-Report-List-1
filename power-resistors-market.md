<p><strong>The Power Resistors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Chassis Mounted</li><li>Surface Mounted</li><li>Through-Hole Mounted</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The power resistors market is categorized into three main types based on their mounting methods: chassis-mounted, surface-mounted, and through-hole mounted. Chassis-mounted resistors are designed for installation on a panel or enclosure, providing robust heat dissipation and mechanical support. Surface-mounted resistors are compact and integrated directly onto PCB surfaces, ideal for space-constrained applications. Through-hole mounted resistors feature leads that pass through holes in a PCB, offering strong mechanical connections and ease of replacement, making them suitable for various electronic devices.</p></p>
