<p><ul><li>Circulating Cell Free DNA (ccfDNA)</li><li>Circulating Tumor Cells (CTCs)</li><li>Exosomes/Extracellular Vesicles</li><li>Circulating Cell Free RNA (ccfRNA) / miRNA</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rare biomarkers market encompasses various types that aid in disease diagnosis and monitoring. Circulating cell-free DNA (ccfDNA) provides insights into genetic alterations from tumors. Circulating tumor cells (CTCs) shed from tumors into the bloodstream enable cancer tracking. Exosomes and extracellular vesicles facilitate intercellular communication, carrying proteins and RNAs that reflect disease states. Circulating cell-free RNA (ccfRNA) and microRNA (miRNA) serve as important indicators of cellular processes and disease, enhancing precision medicine applications.</p></p>
