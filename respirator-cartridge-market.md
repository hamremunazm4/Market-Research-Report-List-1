<p><p>The respirator cartridge market is primarily categorized into combination cartridges and particulate cartridges. In contrast, particulate cartridges focus solely on removing airborne particulates, such as dust, smoke, and aerosols, ensuring respiratory protection in environments with specific particulate hazards.
