<p><ul><li>Fixed POS Terminals</li><li>Mobile POS Terminals</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The restaurant POS terminals market is categorized into fixed and mobile POS terminals. Fixed POS terminals are stationary systems typically found at checkout counters, offering robust features for order processing, inventory management, and payment transactions. In contrast, mobile POS terminals are portable devices that enable transactions anywhere within the restaurant, enhancing customer service by allowing staff to take orders and process payments directly at the table. Both types streamline operations, improve efficiency, and enhance the overall dining experience for customers.</p></p>
