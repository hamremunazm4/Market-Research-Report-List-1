<p><p>The Spring Dampers market encompasses various types, each serving distinct applications. Compression springs absorb energy by resisting axial compression and are widely used in automotive suspensions. Force springs, designed to exert a specific force, are often employed in mechanisms needing consistent pressure. Tension springs store energy when stretched and are vital in applications like door closures. Radial springs manage rotational forces, while garter springs provide circular tension in seals and mechanical systems, showcasing the diversity within the spring dampers segment.</p></p>
