<p><p>The steam retorts market is primarily divided into two types: static retorts and rotary retorts. Static retorts consist of fixed chambers that process food products in stacked trays, ideal for bulk processing and preserving delicate items. In contrast, rotary retorts have a rotating mechanism that ensures even heat distribution, enhancing efficiency and reducing processing times. Both types cater to various industries, focusing on food preservation and safety, while addressing specific production needs and product characteristics.</p></p>
