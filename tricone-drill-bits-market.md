<p><p>Tricone drill bits are widely used in drilling operations, categorized primarily into two types: TCI (Tungsten Carbide Insert) drill bits and milled tooth drill bits. TCI bits feature durable tungsten carbide inserts that enhance performance in hard formations, offering extended life and efficiency. Milled tooth bits, with their steel teeth, are ideal for softer formations and provide cost-effective solutions for various drilling environments. Both types cater to different geological conditions, optimizing drilling effectiveness across the industry.</p></p>
