<p><strong>The Underwater Drone Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>AUV</li><li>ROV</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The underwater drone market is primarily divided into two main types: Autonomous Underwater Vehicles (AUVs) and Remotely Operated Vehicles (ROVs). AUVs are self-guided, capable of operating independently to collect data and perform tasks, making them ideal for deep-sea exploration and mapping. In contrast, ROVs are tethered to a surface vessel and controlled remotely, providing real-time video feedback and precise maneuvering for tasks like inspection, maintenance, and research. Both play crucial roles in marine exploration and various industrial applications.</p></p>
