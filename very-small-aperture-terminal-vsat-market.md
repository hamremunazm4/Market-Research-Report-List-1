The market is divided into two main types: Fixed Stations and Mobile Stations. Fixed Station VSATs are permanently installed at specific locations, often used for remote monitoring, enterprise connectivity, or internet access. In contrast, Mobile Station VSATs offer portability, enabling users to establish communication links in various locations, making them ideal for disaster recovery, military applications, and field operations.</p></p>
