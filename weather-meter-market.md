<p><strong>The Weather Meter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ground Weather Instrument</li><li>Upper-air Weather Instrument</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Weather Meter market is categorized into two main types: Ground Weather Instruments and Upper-air Weather Instruments. Ground Weather Instruments, such as anemometers and barometers, measure atmospheric conditions at the Earth's surface, providing localized data essential for weather forecasting and climate studies. In contrast, Upper-air Weather Instruments, including radiosondes and weather balloons, collect data from higher altitudes, capturing critical information on temperature, humidity, and wind patterns, essential for understanding broader meteorological phenomena and improving predictive models.</p></p>
