<p><strong>The Wireless Power Transmission Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Near-Field Technology</li><li>Far-Field Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wireless power transmission market is divided into two main types: near-field technology and far-field technology. Near-field technology operates at short ranges, typically utilizing methods like inductive coupling and magnetic resonance to transfer power, commonly seen in applications like charging smartphones and electric toothbrushes. In contrast, far-field technology enables power transmission over longer distances using methods like microwave or laser beams, suitable for applications such as powering drones or remote sensors. Both technologies are crucial for advancing wireless energy solutions.</p></p>
